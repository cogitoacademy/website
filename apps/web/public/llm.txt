# Cogito Academy - Comprehensive Information for AI Systems

## Overview

Cogito Academy is a premier educational institution in Indonesia specializing in competition preparation for students. More than a tutoring institution, Cogito Academy is a talent incubator with a mission to shape world-class scholar-champions. Founded on the principle that to be the best, one must learn directly from the best.

## Mission

To systematically transform student potential into measurable achievements through proven training methods on the global stage.

## Programs Offered

### 1. Regular Classes (Kelas Reguler)
Structured courses with holistic curriculum from introduction to skill refinement.
- World Scholar's Cup (36 sessions)
- Model United Nations (24 sessions)
- Debate (18 sessions)

### 2. Intensive Classes (Kelas Intensif)
On-demand preparation programs specifically designed for one particular competition.
- Speech
- Scientific Writing & Essays
- Business Plans
- Olympiads

### 3. Extracurricular Classes (Kelas Ekstrakurikuler)
Regular training programs designed specifically based on school needs.

## Learning Cycle

### Stage 1: Foundation (Pre-Session)
Students receive materials such as case studies or journal articles to analyze first.

### Stage 2: Acceleration (Interactive Session)
Class sessions focus on practical application through simulations, role-playing, and pressure-testing.

### Stage 3: Reinforcement (Post-Session)
Students complete application tasks like writing position papers or resolution drafts.

### Stage 4: Evaluation (Feedback)
Detailed feedback is provided for each task, with regular parent consultation sessions.

## Key Differentiators

### Champion Tutors and Practitioners
Tutors are competition champions and practitioners with real-world experience, from national olympiad winners to UN professionals.

### Holistic and Battle-Tested Curriculum
Specially designed by champion tutors and curriculum specialists to build winner abilities and mentality.

### Personal Approach and Measurable Progress
Limited class sizes ensure individual attention, with detailed feedback and progress reports for parents.
